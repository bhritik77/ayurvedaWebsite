Panchakarma Therapy Program

Panchakarma is Ayurveda’s most powerful detox program. It removes deep-rooted toxins, balances doshas, improves metabolism, and boosts immunity. Our center offers all five procedures:

  • Vamana – Therapeutic emesis
  • Virechana – Purgation therapy
  • Basti – Herbal oil enema
  • Nasya – Nasal cleansing therapy
  • Raktamokshana – Blood detox
